Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans anytime?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference immediately. When downgrading, the change takes effect at your next billing cycle.
Is there a free trial?
Absolutely! All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You'll have full access to all features in your selected plan during the trial period.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), ACH bank transfers, and wire transfers for annual Enterprise plans. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
Is my data HIPAA compliant?
Yes, all plans are built on HIPAA-compliant infrastructure. Enterprise plans include a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) at no additional cost. Essentials and Professional plans can add a BAA for a small monthly fee.
Do you offer discounts for academic or nonprofit sites?
Yes! We offer special pricing for academic research institutions and nonprofit organizations. Contact our sales team to learn more about our academic and nonprofit programs.
What happens if I exceed my plan limits?
We'll notify you when you're approaching your limits. If you exceed them, we'll reach out to discuss upgrading to a plan that better fits your needs. We won't cut off access - we work with you to find the right solution.
